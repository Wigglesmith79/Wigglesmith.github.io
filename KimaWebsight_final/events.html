Events

The Kemah Boardwalk regularly hosts a variety of exciting events that bring together families, friends, and visitors for unforgettable experiences along the waterfront.

These events often feature live music, delicious food from local restaurants, themed festivals, and seasonal celebrations.

Guests can enjoy everything from wine tastings and craft fairs to fireworks shows and outdoor concerts.

With its scenic views, amusement rides, and vibrant atmosphere, the Kemah Boardwalk offers a fun-filled setting for people of all ages to gather and make lasting memories.
