About Kemah Boardwalk

📍 Location

Kemah Boardwalk is located in Kemah, Texas, just 20 miles southeast of Houston, along the scenic Galveston Bay.

🏛️ History

The Kemah Boardwalk opened in 1997 and was developed by Landry’s, Inc.. What started as a small restaurant and retail area has since grown into one of the most beloved entertainment districts on the Gulf Coast, blending coastal charm with modern attractions.

⭐ Key Attractions & Highlights

  • The Boardwalk Bullet – A 96-foot-tall wooden roller coaster
  • Classic Rides – Ferris wheel, carousel, train, and more
  • Aquarium Restaurant – Dine with a 50,000-gallon aquatic tank
  • Boardwalk Beast – High-speed boat thrill ride
  • Live Music & Festivals – Free shows all year long
  • Waterfront Dining – Seafood and American eats with a view
  • Shops & Souvenirs – Candy shops, t-shirts, and coastal keepsakes
