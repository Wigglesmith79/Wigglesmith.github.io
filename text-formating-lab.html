Downwell is a mobile game with random level generation, permadeath and upgrades that has a vertical format where you fall down a hole stomping and shooting enemies building up a combo and getting upgrades. The game has extreemly tight design, because nothing is waisted and every game elemnt including the art has multiple uses


Downwell uses a palette of four colors, which default to black background and white outlines, with red as a highlight for gems, enemies and other important items, and blue for water environment features.[2][3] The exact colors can be changed through unlockable in-game palettes.[4] The art style and underground level design have been compared to Spelunky and Cave Story.[5] - wikipedia
